"Be Where the world is going"

Linuex - India's first instant messaging app (Linuex is a cross platform encrypted messaging service developed by Linuex Foundation. It allow user to send text and voice message , make voice and video call and share Images, documents,user location and other media . Also it gives you entertainment facility like reels you can disable or enable that functionality according to user convince)

End to end encryption - The message travels all the way from the sender to the recipient in encrypted form .
Share anything - high-speed capability of file sharing without any doubt. linuex is a cross-platform software that allows users to share files from Android, iOS, Windows PC, and Mac
Get Together with Groups - Group chats make it easy to stay connected to your family, friends, and coworkers.
