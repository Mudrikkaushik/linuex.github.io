Information we collect

We may require particular information, e.g.

  • Account Information. You register a phone number when you create a Linuex account. Phone numbers are used to provide our Services to you and other Linuex users. You may optionally add other information to your account, such as a profile name and profile picture. This information is end-to-end encrypted.

  • Messages. Linuex cannot decrypt or otherwise access the content of your messages or calls. Linuex queues end-to-end encrypted messages on its servers for delivery to devices that are temporarily offline (e.g. a phone whose battery has died). Your message history is stored on your own devices.

  • Contacts. Linuex can optionally discover which contacts in your address book are Linuex users, using a service designed to protect the privacy of your contacts. Information from the contacts on your device may be cryptographically hashed and transmitted to the server in order to determine which of your contacts are registered.

  • User Support.If you contact Linuex User Support, any personal data you may share with us is kept only for the purposes of researching the issue and contacting you about your case.
